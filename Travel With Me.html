Travel With Me!

Next Solo Trip: Spain & Portugal- April 2024

Day 1: Arrival in Lisbon, Portugal

-Arrive in Lisbon and settle into your hostel/ hotel.

-Explore the historic Alfama district and enjoy traditional Portuguese cuisine.

Day 2: Lisbon

-Visit the Belem Tower and Jeronimos Monastery.

-Stroll along the waterfront and enjoy a Pastel de Nata.

-Explore the LX Factory for its art and culture.

Day 3: Sintra day trip

-Take a day trip to Sintra to visit the colorful Pena Palace and the Moorish Castle.

-Explore the charming town of Sintra.

Day 4: Lisbon to Porto

-Travel to Porto by train or bus.

- Porto's historic center and visit Livraria Lello and the Clerigos Tower.

Day 5: Porto

-Explore the Ribeira district and take a boat tour on the Douro River.

-Visit a port wine cellar for a tasting.

Day 6: Coimbra day trip

-Take a day trip to Coimbra to visit the historic university and the beautiful Joanina Library.

-Return to Porto in the evening.

Day 7: Porto to Santiago de Compostela, Spain

-Travel to Santiago de Compostela by train or bus (about 4 hours).

-Visit the Santiago de Compostela Cathedral and the old town.

Day 8: Santiago de Compostela to A Coruña day trip

-Take a day trip to A Coruña to visit the Tower of Hercules and the beaches. Don't forget your bathing suit!

-Head to Santiago de Compostela in the evening.

Day 9: Madrid, Spain

-Travel to Madrid by train or bus.

-Explore the Prado Museum, Puerta del Sol, and Retiro Park.

-Day 10: Madrid

-Visit the Royal Palace of Madrid and explore the historic district, go grab a pastry from a cafe!

-Enjoy some authentic Spanish cuisine for your last evening in Spain.
